Why we call ourselves Dwellers

The Crack Shack is larger than any one virtual. It is the inventive community of dwellers and their belief that our reality is just one system among many in the virtual world that brought the endevour of Crack Shack

The Crack Shack, the registrants, called migrants, would ascend from the SystemSpace (Life) to the next system (LFE) upon their death. Users were warned not to die before the date of the ascension and not to commit suicide, otherwise their souls would not transfer from Life to LFE.

We collectively decided that we would not stand idly by and let our community wither. Creating this website is our best means of ensuring her continued growth and longevity.
